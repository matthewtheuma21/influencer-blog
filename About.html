ABOUT ME

Whats up? Im Antonino, an italian native influencer from Ragusa, Sicilia. I have always loved to explore and travel around different countries, learn and experience new stuff everyday! I take my adventures seriously, capturing every moment or scenary with a story to tell, and just enjoy my time. I usually go abroad alone, because it allows me to have my space and be liberal. But its not just travelling, I won the 'Best Male Tennis Player' in 2017, tennis is my sport passion. I like to have fun with my friends, take on boat rides and dive into the mystery of the sea. Other hobbies I have is modelling, cycling, reading and more.

As an influencer, my goal is to build a community of independent travellers seeking to unlock richer travel experiences; helping the curious discover less-visited places in popular destinations, and rewarding adventures in more remote locations.

If you would like to contact me, you can follow through my Social Media at the top.
